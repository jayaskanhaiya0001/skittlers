FAQs

What is an animated explainer video?
An explainer animated video is a short duration video wherein, the complex and important aspects related to business are demonstrated in a simple as well as engaging manner. In the span of 2 minutes, the High Definition video is shot covering animated version of problem solving approach for increasing brand awareness.
How much does an explainer video cost?
Even though, there are many online tools and resources available for the customization of explainer video, but, the best way to get the effective outcome is to hire the professional explainer video production team. In terms of the cost of explainer video, it can be stated that the price is estimated on the basis of intricacies of the production services used. For instance, the average cost of tool based animation is about INR 20,000 per minute, semi customized animated video costs almost INR 42,000 and fully customized animated explainer video costs up to INR 60,000. But, the exact pricing can be evaluated on the basis of business requirements and details to be used in the script of the video.
How long should an explainer video be?
The purpose of an explainer video is to engage the target audience in a short duration. Thus, the ideal duration of the video should be less than 1 minute. However, if the aspects to be elaborated in the video are multiple, then, within the span of 60-90 minutes or 2 minutes, the same should be covered. In fact, Skittles Productions has a wide range of effective explainer video production tools which make the final outcome engaging and simple to understand.
